User Manual

  • Loading ml5 library may take a few seconds before buttons can be clicked.
  • Image recognition largely depends on the training data it has, so new players should train a new model by clicking 'New Model'.
  • To train the model, players can view the on-screen video feedback and click label buttons ('Up', 'Down', etc.) accordingly. More models mean better recognition results.
  • The mini project is still very much of a prototype.
  • Thank you for giving it a try.
